Our Founding Story

Phonics Link was created to help parents find reading resources for their kids.

Kali and Anika had to make an educational website for their Information Technologies course at Pratt Institute. After a few minutes of brainstorming, and A LOT OF TIME making this website, Phonics Link was born!
